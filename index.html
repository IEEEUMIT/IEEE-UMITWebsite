Who we are

IEEE is the world's largest professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.IEEE and its members inspire a global community through IEEE's highly cited publications, conferences,technology standards, and professional and educational activities.

Benefits of IEEE Membership

IEEE membership offers access to technical innovation, cutting-edge information, networking opportunities, and exclusive member benefits.

  • Members support IEEE's mission to advance technology for humanity and the profession, while memberships build a platform to introduce careers in technology to students around the world.
  • IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.

Women in Engineering

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network of IEEE members and volunteers dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering and science.

  • Provide assistance with the formation of new IEEE WIE Affinity Groups and support ongoing activities.
  • Recognize the outstanding achievements of women in electrical and electronics engineering through IEEE Awards nominations.
  • Advocate women in leadership roles in IEEE governance and career advancement for women in the profession
  • Organize workshops at major technical conferences to enhance networking and to promote membership in IEEE WIE.

Events

IEEE Day

IEEE Day ia a global event to celebrate everywhere around the world the achievements of IEEE members.This Day is celebrated every year on the first Tuesday of October.

IOT Workshop

This is the event that was conducted in UMIT in 2019-20.It was a 2 day workshop for the students to explore and learn about IOT.

Industrial Visit

The most awaited throughout the year is the industrial visit.IEEE UMIT arranges this visit in the northern states of India.

IEEE Fest

The main event of the entire year is the IEEE Fest.It is organized by the council members with dedication and creativity.

Hackathon

Hackathon was conducted for the very first time in UMIT last year.It was a great success and the students very able to exlore more in field of technology having their fair share of fun.

IEEE GRSS Event

IEEE UMIT in collaboration with IEEE GRSS conducted an event on geotechnology.

AI Gaming Event

AI Gaming event was organized in year 2020.It was a 2 day event wherin students learnt about python and made a game on their own.

Among Us

Technophilia
